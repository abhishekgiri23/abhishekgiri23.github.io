Ergonomic Care Height

To protect the nurse's back, your bed, changing table (*commode*), and bath stand must be at least **80 cm** high.

  • • Measured floor to mattress top
  • • Borrow 'Bedverhogers' (Klossen) for free
  • • Must be ready by Week 37
🛌

Safe Sleep Rules

Guidelines to prevent accidents and SIDS in the first critical weeks.

NO AEROSLEEP TOPPERS

In the first week with metal bottles (*kruiken*), toppers prevent water from draining, risking burns.

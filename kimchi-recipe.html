2. The Preparation Journey

1

Prep & Salt

Cut cucumbers into bite-sized pieces. Salt generously and wait 30 minutes. This draws out water to ensure a crispy texture.

2

The Big Squeeze

Drain the liquid and squeeze cucumbers firmly. Do not rinse! Add your chopped onions and julienned carrots to the mix.

3

Paste & Infuse

Mix Gochugaru, oil, soy, garlic, sugar, and seeds. Toss everything together. Chill for 1-3 days for deep flavor.

💡 Pro-Tip: The Science of Squeezing

Salting creates an osmotic pressure that pulls moisture out of the cucumber cells. If you skip the squeeze, that water will release into your sauce later, diluting the flavor and making the cucumbers soggy. Never rinse—you want that saltiness to remain part of the base seasoning!

❄️ Storage & Maturation

  • • Keep refrigerated in an airtight container immediately.
  • • Day 1: Fresh, salad-like, spicy.
  • • Day 3: Peak flavor profile (fermentation starts).
  • • Best consumed within 4 days for maximum crunch.
